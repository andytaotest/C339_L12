Full-time Diplomas

Pursue your passion at RP. With our wide array of courses, you’re sure to find your dream course at RP.

Xperiential Learning Centre

Embrace the adventure at RP's Xperiential Learning Centre (XLC)! Featuring 10 cutting-edge facilities and an auto-belay system, XLC promises a weather-proof and inclusive adventure for everyone, including wheelchair users. 

Minor Programmes

With our Minor Programmes, you can be so much more! Learn how you can graduate with a minor in Business, Sustainability or Digitalisation on top of your diploma.

Unique Experience

Cool CCAs

Chase your interests or explore new activities with over 80 co-curricular activities (CCAs) to choose from, including sports, adventure, service-learning, and the arts.

Ready to take the leap and reach your next career milestone? At our Academy for Continuing Education, you’ll deepen your knowledge, take ownership of your learning, and master skills that will give you an edge.
